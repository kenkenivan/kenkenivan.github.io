Featured Projects

Interactive and responsive systems designed with modern technologies.

01

Thesis Gate

Thesis Gate is a web-based thesis management system designed to improve the accessibility, organization, and retrieval of research papers for students and faculty members. The system allows users to upload, browse, and search thesis documents efficiently through a centralized digital platform. It aims to reduce manual handling of research archives while providing a faster and more convenient way of accessing academic resources for Computer Engineering students and researchers.

02

SKIDS System

SKIDS is a web-based information dissemination system developed to improve communication between the Sangguniang Kabataan and the youth in the community. The system provides easy access to announcements, event schedules, approved resolutions, budget reports, and youth information through a centralized digital platform. It aims to promote transparency, accessibility, and efficient distribution of information while encouraging greater youth engagement and participation in barangay activities.

03

Face Detection AI

Face Detection AI is a computer vision-based system that uses artificial intelligence to detect and identify human faces in real time through a webcam or camera input. The system processes visual data and highlights detected faces using bounding boxes, demonstrating the application of AI and image processing technologies. It can be used in various fields such as security, attendance monitoring, surveillance, and smart automation systems, showcasing the potential of machine learning and computer vision in modern technology.
